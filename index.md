## On-line Courses

Ten courses covering the full stack from SQL fundamentals to cutting-edge agentic AI — building the theoretical backbone behind the capstone projects.

**Tracks:** `🗄️ Database` `🤖 AI` `📊 Data`

### Foundations *(sampling only — not completed in full)*

**[Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate](https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/google-data-analytics)**

`📊 Data` `🔍 Partial`

A 9-course deep dive into the full data analyst workflow — cleaning, analysing, and visualising data with SQL, Python, and Tableau. Recognised by 150+ employers; 75% of graduates report positive career outcomes within six months.

⏱ ~6 months · 10 hrs/week


**[IBM Data Science Professional Certificate](https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/ibm-data-science)**

`📊 Data` `🗄️ Database` `🔍 Partial`

A 12-course series covering the full data scientist toolkit — Python, SQL, data visualisation, and machine learning — built around real-world projects that form a job-ready portfolio. Backed by IBM's Talent Network for job matching after completion.

⏱ ~4 months · 10 hrs/week


### Core Curriculum

**[Databases and SQL for Data Science with Python](https://www.coursera.org/learn/sql-data-science)**

`🗄️ Database`

Write SQL from basic SELECTs all the way up to joins, views, and transactions, then drive real cloud databases straight from Python in Jupyter notebooks.

⏱ ~2 weeks · 10 hrs/week


**[Data Analysis with Python](https://www.coursera.org/learn/data-analysis-with-python)**

`📊 Data`

End-to-end analysis using Pandas, NumPy, and Scikit-learn: import messy data, clean it, explore it statistically, then build and evaluate predictive regression models.

⏱ ~2 weeks · 10 hrs/week


**[Data Visualization with Python](https://www.coursera.org/learn/python-for-data-visualization)**

`📊 Data`

Hands-on use of Matplotlib, Seaborn, Folium, Plotly, and Dash to create everything from basic charts to fully interactive dashboards — the visual storytelling layer on top of your data analysis work.

⏱ ~2 weeks · 10 hrs/week


**[Generative AI with Large Language Models](https://www.coursera.org/learn/generative-ai-with-llms)**

`🤖 AI`

The full LLM lifecycle in one course: transformer architecture, training, fine-tuning with LoRA and soft prompts, and alignment via RLHF. Co-created by AWS and DeepLearning.AI.

⏱ ~3 weeks · 10 hrs/week


**[Applied Generative AI & NLP with Python](https://www.coursera.org/learn/packt-applied-generative-ai-and-natural-language-processing-with-python-unemz)**

`🤖 AI`

Build real NLP applications with Hugging Face and OpenAI — sentiment analysis, text classification, fine-tuning, vector databases, and advanced chain-of-thought prompting.

⏱ ~1 week · 10 hrs/week


**[Building Systems with the ChatGPT API](https://www.coursera.org/projects/building-systems-with-the-chatgpt-api-project)**

`🤖 AI`

A hands-on guided project where you chain LLM calls, classify queries, evaluate outputs for safety, and ship a production-ready customer service chatbot in Python.

⏱ ~1 hour


**[CrewAI Tools, MCP, and Agentic RAG](https://www.coursera.org/learn/crewai-tools-mcp-agentic-rag)**

`🤖 AI`

Build secure multi-agent systems with CrewAI: tool integration, memory, retrieval-augmented generation, and the Model Context Protocol — the architecture behind the capstone agent projects.

⏱ ~1 week · 10 hrs/week


**[LangChain: Chat with Your Data](https://www.coursera.org/projects/langchain-chat-with-your-data-project)**

`🤖 AI`

A focused guided project on building RAG pipelines with LangChain — document loading, splitting, embedding, vector storage, and conversational retrieval chains over your own data.

⏱ ~2 hours

## Capstone Projects

Twelve AI-powered tools built around real, everyday problems — each one designed to be something you'd actually reach for, not a throwaway demo. Each project runs **6–7 weeks** at a minimum of **10 hours per week**, balancing design, implementation, and real-world testing.

**[Dynamic Vibe-Matcher](projects/dynamic-vibe-matcher)**

`🤖 AI` `🎵 Music`

Filters your Spotify playlists down to match your mood, described in plain English.


**[Family Recipe Intelligence System](projects/family-recipe-intelligence-system)**

`🤖 AI` `🍳 Lifestyle`

Turns scattered family recipes into a smart cookbook you can query by ingredients.


**[Friend-Group Vibe Compromiser](projects/friend-group-vibe-compromiser)**

`🤖 AI` `🎵 Music`

Blends everyone's playlist tastes into one playlist that fits the group and the occasion.


**[Ledger Lens - Private Transaction Categorizer & Reporter](projects/ledger-lens-private-transaction-categorizer-and-reporter)**

`🤖 AI` `💰 Finance`

Parses messy bank statements into a unified ledger with automatic categorization and reports.


**[Reddit Morning Brief](projects/reddit-morning-brief)**

`🤖 AI` `📰 Productivity`

Summarizes your chosen subreddits daily so you get what matters without the scroll.


**[Resume Match - Job Fit Analyzer](projects/resume-match-job-fit-analyzer)**

`🤖 AI` `💼 Career`

Scores your resume against a job posting and tells you exactly where the gaps are.


**[Smart List Watcher](projects/smart-list-watcher)**

`🤖 AI` `📰 Productivity`

Watches any listing site and alerts you only when a new item genuinely matches what you described.


**[Smart Playlist Shifter - Activity Pacer](projects/smart-playlist-shifter-activity-pacer)**

`🤖 AI` `🎵 Music`

Reorders a playlist to follow the energy arc of your workout, study session, or activity.


**[Spotify-to-YouTube Bridge with AI Playlist Builder](projects/spotify-to-youtube-bridge-with-ai-playlist-builder)**

`🤖 AI` `🎵 Music`

Converts a Spotify playlist to YouTube links and extends it with AI-suggested songs that fit the vibe.


**[SQL-Insight - Natural Language Data Explorer](projects/sql-insight-natural-language-data-explorer)**

`🤖 AI` `🗄️ Database`

Lets anyone query a real database in plain English and get back tables, charts, and the SQL behind them.


**[StudyStack - Adaptive Flashcard Engine](projects/studystack-adaptive-flashcard-engine)**

`🤖 AI` `📰 Productivity`

Turns your notes into smart flashcards that focus your review on what you actually struggle with.


**[Subscription Overlap Auditor - Family Spending Helper](projects/subscription-overlap-auditor-family-spending-helper)**

`🤖 AI` `💰 Finance`

Scans family bank statements to surface duplicate subscriptions and show where money is being wasted.
