# Capstone Projects

Twelve AI-powered tools built around real, everyday problems — each one designed to be something you'd actually reach for, not a throwaway demo.

- [Dynamic Vibe-Matcher](projects/dynamic-vibe-matcher) — Filters your Spotify playlists down to match your mood, described in plain English.
- [Family Recipe Intelligence System](projects/family-recipe-intelligence-system) — Turns scattered family recipes into a smart cookbook you can query by ingredients.
- [Friend-Group Vibe Compromiser](projects/friend-group-vibe-compromiser) — Blends everyone's playlist tastes into one playlist that fits the group and the occasion.
- [Ledger Lens - Private Transaction Categorizer & Reporter](projects/ledger-lens-private-transaction-categorizer-and-reporter) — Parses messy bank statements into a unified ledger with automatic categorization and reports.
- [Reddit Morning Brief](projects/reddit-morning-brief) — Summarizes your chosen subreddits daily so you get what matters without the scroll.
- [Resume Match - Job Fit Analyzer](projects/resume-match-job-fit-analyzer) — Scores your resume against a job posting and tells you exactly where the gaps are.
- [Smart List Watcher](projects/smart-list-watcher) — Watches any listing site and alerts you only when a new item genuinely matches what you described.
- [Smart Playlist Shifter - Activity Pacer](projects/smart-playlist-shifter-activity-pacer) — Reorders a playlist to follow the energy arc of your workout, study session, or activity.
- [Spotify-to-YouTube Bridge with AI Playlist Builder](projects/spotify-to-youtube-bridge-with-ai-playlist-builder) — Converts a Spotify playlist to YouTube links and extends it with AI-suggested songs that fit the vibe.
- [SQL-Insight - Natural Language Data Explorer](projects/sql-insight-natural-language-data-explorer) — Lets anyone query a real database in plain English and get back tables, charts, and the SQL behind them.
- [StudyStack - Adaptive Flashcard Engine](projects/studystack-adaptive-flashcard-engine) — Turns your notes into smart flashcards that focus your review on what you actually struggle with.
- [Subscription Overlap Auditor - Family Spending Helper](projects/subscription-overlap-auditor-family-spending-helper) — Scans family bank statements to surface duplicate subscriptions and show where money is being wasted.
